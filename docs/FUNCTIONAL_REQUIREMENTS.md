# Functional Requirements

Version: 1.0

---

## FR-001
System shall connect to one or more cameras.

Priority: Critical

---

## FR-002
System shall automatically reconnect if a camera disconnects.

Priority: Critical

---

## FR-003
System shall record video from every camera.

Priority: High

---

## FR-004
System shall support simulation mode using recorded videos.

Priority: High

---

## FR-005
System shall detect birds on the weighing platform.

Priority: Critical

---

## FR-006
System shall count birds entering the weighing platform.

Priority: Critical

---

## FR-007
System shall count birds leaving the weighing platform.

Priority: Critical

---

## FR-008
System shall track birds across frames.

Priority: Critical

---

## FR-009
System shall read the weight machine display.

Priority: Critical

---

## FR-010
System shall detect the DONE status.

Priority: Critical

---

## FR-011
System shall read batch number.

Priority: High

---

## FR-012
System shall generate loading events.

Priority: Critical

---

## FR-013
System shall detect birds removed before DONE.

Priority: Critical

---

## FR-014
System shall save evidence clips for critical events.

Priority: Critical

---

## FR-015
System shall store every event in the database.

Priority: Critical

---

## FR-016
System shall display live camera feeds.

Priority: High

---

## FR-017
System shall generate loading reports.

Priority: High

---

## FR-018
System shall work without internet.

Priority: Critical

---

## FR-019
System shall synchronize data when internet becomes available.

Priority: Medium

---

## FR-020
System shall support multiple farms in future versions.

Priority: Medium
